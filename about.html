About our Restaurant

The Restaurant

Tom Sellers is the Chef Patron of The Eatout. He dreamed of opening The Eatout at the age of 19, and in April 2013, aged 26, was able to realise his dream. Tom trained at some of the world’s best restaurants including Tom Aikens, Noma and Per Se. His food is predominantly British, using seasonality to lead a menu that invokes memory and brings a narrative to each plate.

Tom joined The Eatout in late 2017 his career has taken him through the kitchens of The Ritz in London, Per Se in New York and L’enclume in the Lake District. In 2019 Tom competed in The World Finals of the Bocuse D’or, finishing 10th in the world. With a deep passion for British products and a keen eye for detail Tom is now Head Chef at The Eatout..
