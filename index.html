Welcome to The Eatout in Sandton

The Eatout Restaurant

The look and feel of the restaurant is a soulful yet stylish space which resonates with the owners own values. The space is perfect for a relaxed business lunch, but is elevated with designer imported lighting into the perfect spot for afternoon champagne with friends. The restaurant’s dining area is divided into different sections. Guests can enjoy a sit-down meal at one of the bespoke dining tables, relax with a drink in the Nest Bar, or take a seat at the contemporary sushi bar. Next to the bar is a featured walk-through, arched wine cellar displaying over 200 bottles of wine, a firm favourite for wine connoisseurs..
